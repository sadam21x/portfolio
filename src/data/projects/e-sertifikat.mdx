Certificates are an important part of one's educational journey. However, the process of creating and managing certificates can be complex and time-consuming. Introducing, __E-Sertifikat__, a web-based application that can help campus authorities in creating, managing, and distributing digital certificates to students.

Core Features:
- Certificate Creation
- Certificate Management
- Certificate Distribution

Tech-Stack:
- PHP
- Laravel
