__LayanObat__ is an innovative pharmacy management application designed to simplify the process of managing your pharmacy. With its powerful features, LayanObat makes it easy for pharmacists and pharmacy owners to keep track of inventory, sales, and customer data.

Core Features:
- Inventory Management
- Point of Sales (POS)
- Report Generation
- Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- Cloud Synchronization

Tech-Stack:
- Go
- Java
- PHP
- Laravel
